## 摘要
我们显式构造了两个 2-生成子群 $H,K\leq \SL(3,\Z)$，其交并非有限生成。该构造发生在标准抛物子群 $\Z^2\rtimes \SL(2,\Z)\leq \SL(3,\Z)$ 内部。关键点在于将 $H\cap K$ 等同于自由群在 $\Z^2$ 上的仿射作用的某个点的稳定子群，然后利用该作用的 Schreier 图证明该稳定子群不是有限生成的。进一步，我们证明存在一列子群 $H_q, K_q \leq \SL(3,\mathbb{Z})$ 使得 $\rank(H_q)=\rank(K_q)=4$，且 \[ \rank(H_q\cap K_q)\geq q+1, \] 同时 $H_q\cap K_q$ 是有限生成的。

## Abstract
We give an explicit construction of two $2$-generated subgroups $H,K\leq \SL(3,\Z)$ whose intersection is not finitely generated. The construction takes place inside the standard parabolic subgroup $\Z^2\rtimes \SL(2,\Z)\leq \SL(3,\Z)$. The main point is to identify $H\cap K$ with the stabilizer of a point for an affine action of a free group on $\Z^2$, and then to prove, using the Schreier graph of this action, that this stabilizer is not finitely generated. Furthermore, we prove that there exists a sequence of subgroups $H_q, K_q \leq \SL(3,\mathbb{Z})$ such that $\rank(H_q)=\rank(K_q)=4$, and \[ \rank(H_q\cap K_q)\geq q+1, \] while $H_q\cap K_q$ is finitely generated.
